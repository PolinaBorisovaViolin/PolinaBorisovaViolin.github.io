Biography

. Winner of more than twenty international competitions violinist Polina Borisova combines expressive musicality, brilliant technique, diverse repertoire and knowledge of different music styles. Giving solo recitals and masterclasses across the globe brought her international recognition and gave experience that she has been successfully sharing with colleagues and younger generation.
. Born in Russia in a musical family she started her violin lessons at the age of 4 and already at 5 gave her first solo recital. Later, she received her master’s degree with summa cum laude and PhD from the Moscow State Conservatory, where she studied with David’s Oistrakh student Professor Ara Bogdanyan. Her teachers include Sergey Krylov and Boris Kushnir.
. Since 2013, Polina is an Assistant Professor of the violin at the Moscow State Conservatory. Her students have been successfully participating in competitions achieving highest results, prize awards and winning orchestra auditions. Since 2017, she is a regular guest soloist with the Moscow Philharmonic Society.
. Her performance highlights include solo appearances with famous orchestras such as St. Petersburg State Symphony Orchestra, Moscow Chamber Orchestra “Music Viva” (was streamed on the radio “Orpheus”), Philharmonic Orchestras of Samara, Astrakhan, Ryazan and others. Moreover, Mrs. Borisova has been given recitals across Spain including the “Palau de la Musica” concert hall, France, Austria, Italy, Belgium, Poland, Portugal, Malaysia, Slovakia, Vietnam. Polina Borisova has received top prizes and Grand-Prix at many international competitions such as International Balys Dvarionas Competition (Lithuania), International Violin Competition (Armenia), International Competition “The Night in Madrid” (Spain), Agustin Aponte International Competition (Spain), Jan Sibelius Instrumental Music Competition (Finland) etc. Using innovation approach and looking for new colors in classical music, Polina was a founder member of various ensembles. In collaboration with harp, guitar, saxophone and bayan she regularly creates new programs including unique arrangements and compositions of famous contemporary composers. All these programs have had a great success in the best philharmonic halls of Russia and Europe.
